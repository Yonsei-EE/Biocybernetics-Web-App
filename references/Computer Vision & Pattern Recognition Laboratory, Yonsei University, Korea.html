About us

  • Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition(CVPR) Lab began in 1995 by Hyeran Byun, currently a tenured professor in the department of Computer Science at Yonsei University, South Korea.
  • Research areas include, but not limited to, Deep-learning, Intelligent Vehicle, Scene Understanding, face detection and modeling, duplicate video detection, image understanding, gesture recognition, intelligent surveillance, image & video segmentation, and 3D multi-view video processing. We modestly boast alumni, 15 Ph.D. and 62 M.S., who are distinguishing themselves in the industrial and academic world.
