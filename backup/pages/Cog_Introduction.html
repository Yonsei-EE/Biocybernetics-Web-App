- Introduction

      Our research interests are Biologically Inspired Robotics, Cognitive Robotics, Aritificial Life, Animal Cognition, Neural Networks and Neuroethology. We are interested in building the  biophysical or neuralmodel for sensori-motor mechanism of animals, and understanding particular animal behaviours. We find many good applications from this knowledge and design robotic systems (biologically inspired robotics). We also investigate bio-inspired network robots with wireless communications to test their application in real world.
